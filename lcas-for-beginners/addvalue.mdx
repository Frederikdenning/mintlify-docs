<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Communicate with confidence" icon="trumpet" iconType="light">
    Use verified results to support marketing and sales. This reduces the risk of greenwashing and makes messages clearer and more credible.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Comparative LCAs" icon="circle" iconType="light">
    Compare one product against another, or different versions of the same product. For public claims, results should follow ISO standards and ideally be supported by third-party review.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Hotspot analysis" icon="fire" iconType="light">
    Identify the few drivers that create most of the environmental impact and cost. Focus on materials, processes, energy, logistics, or end-of-life.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Design & R&D guidance" iconType="light">
    Test alternative materials, suppliers, or processes early in development. Early choices are cheaper to change and have a bigger effect on both footprint and costs.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## From insight to action

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify top 3 hotspots">
    Map out which materials, suppliers, or processes dominate the footprint.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick high-leverage changes">
    Focus on design tweaks, sourcing shifts, packaging or transport optimizations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate and communicate">
    Recalculate, check trade-offs beyond carbon, and prepare claim wording or an EPD.
  </Step>
</Steps>
