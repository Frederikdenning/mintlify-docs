## How Young Brands Use LCAs in Practice

### 1. **Material Evaluation**
Choosing between organic cotton, recycled polyester, or Tencel requires quantified comparisons. LCAs show differences in carbon footprint, energy demand, and water use â€” not just marketing claims.

### 2. **Supplier Assessment**
LCAs allow small brands to evaluate supply chain partners transparently. By linking supplier data into models, teams can identify where emissions or waste reduction matter most.

### 3. **Regulatory Compliance**
Upcoming EU rules â€” especially the **Green Claims Directive** and **Product Environmental Footprint (PEF)** framework â€” require evidence for environmental statements.  
Brands using LCAs are already aligned with these standards.
