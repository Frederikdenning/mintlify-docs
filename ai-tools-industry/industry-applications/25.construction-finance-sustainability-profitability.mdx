## Why Impact Data Matters for Construction Finance

Construction is one of the most resource-intensive sectors, and environmental inefficiencies often become financial liabilities.  
From embodied emissions in materials to future carbon taxes, the industry faces a cost landscape that rewards transparency and penalizes uncertainty.

Sustainability insights help finance leaders:

- Understand the footprint behind materials and project designs  
- Forecast how carbon pricing or regulation will affect budgets  
- Strengthen ESG and CSRD reporting  
- Identify cost-saving opportunities before construction begins  
- Support more competitive tender submissions
