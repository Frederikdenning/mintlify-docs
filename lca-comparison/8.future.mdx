---
title: "Future of LCA"
---

The future of LCA software looks promising, with advancements in AI and big data analytics on the horizon. These new capabilities will enable real-time assessments and even predictive insights, helping companies make quick, impactful decisions to reduce their environmental impact. With sustainability reporting becoming more regulated, LCA software will be essential for compliance, making it even more critical to choose a tool that aligns with your long-term goals. Sustainly is constantly adding new features, in order to make LCAs easier to perform, and enabling as many stakeholders in a company to utilise the data as possible.
