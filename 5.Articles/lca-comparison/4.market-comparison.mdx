    **SimaPro**

    **Best for:**

    LCA professionals, research institutions

    **Overview:**

    SimaPro is customizable, and has traditionally been used a lot in research.

    ✅**Pros:** Customisable, proven software, supports    many databases.\
    ❌**Cons:** Difficult to scale, limited collaboration, very complex.

    **LCA for experts (GaBi)**

    **Best for:**

    Experts who need access to their proprietary databases\
    **Overview:**

    LCA for experts combines LCA modelling with rich, sector-specific proprietary databases.

    ✅**Pros:** Extensive databases, multiple user types, some collaboration.\
    ❌**Cons:** Expensive, very complex, difficult to scale.

    **OpenLCA**

    \
    **Best for:**

    Those looking for a free, open-source option\
    **Overview:**

    OpenLCA is the only open-source, professional LCA tool. Best for solo practitioners that don’t mind manual steps.

    ✅**Pros:** Free to use, highly flexible, and fully transparent\
    ❌**Cons:** Complex interface, doesn’t scale, not collaborative.
  </Card>

    **Sustainly**

    **Best for:**

    Companies that need scalable and transparent LCAs enabled by AI\
    **Overview:**

    Sustainly uses AI to allow anyone to get started with LCAs and it supercharges experts to deliver results much faster.\
    ✅**Pros:** Best AI in the market, modern interface, scalable data structure, high customisation.\
    ❌**Cons:** Not hyper specialised within one market segment.
  </Card>

    **OneClick LCA**

    **Best for:**

    The construction industry and companies without LCA experts\
    **Overview:**

    This tool is tailored for construction, allowing companies to conduct automated LCA and Environmental Product Declarations (EPD) that align with industry standards.

    \
    ✅**Pros:** Very automated, scalable, automatically align with standards.\
    ❌**Cons:** A black box, no flexibility or customisation, limited data quality due to automation; not easily learned for beginners.
