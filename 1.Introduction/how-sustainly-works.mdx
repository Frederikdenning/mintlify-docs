---
title: "How Sustainly Works"
description: "See how Sustainly turns raw data into audit ready LCA results, from ingestion and model generation to impact calculation, comparisons, and EPD ready reporting."
---

---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Data Ingestion">
    - Import material, process, supplier, and operational data via spreadsheet, API, or integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Model Generation">
    - AI + templated logic convert inputs into a structured life cycle model.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Impact Calculation">
    - Choose methods (e.g. EF, IPCC, ReCiPe), allocation approach, and compute results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Iterate & Compare">
    - Explore design variants, supply chain choices, or sensitivity analyses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reporting & Communication">
    - Export dashboards, EPD tables, or summaries. Invite verifiers or export data sets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Maintenance">
    - Centralized data management ensures easy updates across products.
  </Step>
</Steps>
